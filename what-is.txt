## 🧿 D2R Market – Offline Auction House for Diablo II: Resurrected

Tired of managing your stash manually or losing track of valuable items?

**D2R Market** is a lightweight offline tool that brings an *Auction House experience* to Diablo II: Resurrected single-player.

---

## ⚡ What it does

* 📦 Scan your shared stash automatically
* 🛒 Buy items from a built-in market
* 💰 List your own items for sale
* 🧮 Manage a simple token-based economy
* ⚙️ Works completely offline

---

## 🎯 Why use it?

* No more manual stash tracking
* Makes single-player feel more dynamic
* Adds a sense of progression and trading
* Designed specifically for solo players

---

## 🖥️ How it works

1. Set your D2R save folder
2. Load your shared stash
3. Browse the market or list your items
4. Buy, sell, and manage your inventory

---

## 🧩 Features

* Rune market with tier system
* Shared stash reader
* Item listing system
* Token balance system
* Clean and simple UI
